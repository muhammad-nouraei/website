Transforming Daily Tasks into Rewarding Adventures

QuestPhone was founded by Nethical in April 2024, an app initially with the name DigipauseFull and later rebranded to QuestPhone.

Our Mission

Digital Wellness

We believe in fostering a healthy relationship with technology. Our mission is to help people regain control over their screen time without completely disconnecting from the digital world.

Habit Formation

Through strategic gamification and reward mechanisms, we transform mundane tasks into engaging challenges that help build lasting positive habits.

AI-Powered Progress

Our advanced AI system ensures accountability while making the process of tracking and verifying task completion effortless and engaging.

User Empowerment

We empower users to take control of their time and productivity by providing them with tools that make self-improvement feel natural and rewarding.
